# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Risk managers and quantitative research operators reviewing a multi-strategy,
research-only combined portfolio. They need to verify operational truth,
membership state, data quality, and pending human decisions quickly.

## Product Purpose

Provide one reliable operational view of the Combined Portfolio while keeping
research evidence, simulated execution, and live allocation status explicit.
Success means users can identify what is executed, what is approved but
pending, what is unavailable, and which source supports every displayed value.

## Brand Personality

Institutional, precise, restrained.

## Anti-references

Retail brokerage dashboards, decorative chart galleries, generic SaaS admin
templates, unexplained risk scores, and interfaces that imply live trading or
external market activity when none exists.

## Design Principles

1. Operational truth before visual polish.
2. Dense information must remain scannable and clearly grouped.
3. Missing, pending, unavailable, and executed are distinct states.
4. Human review and execution authority are always explicit.
5. Current operational data never silently falls back to legacy proxy data.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA contrast, keyboard-visible focus states, semantic status
labels that do not rely on color alone, and reduced-motion support.
